Mac and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and Cheese, most commonly known as Mac and Cheese is a dish made up of boiled noodles and various cheeses. You can make it more creamy by adding a bit of milk or more flavorful by experementing with different cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Elbow Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Water

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Boil water until tender
  3. Drain water from noodles
  4. Add cheese and stil
